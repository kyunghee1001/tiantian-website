About Tiantian

Hello, I'm Kyung Hee, the owner of Tiantian. We specialize in wholesale and retail of cosmetic products. With an office, a warehouse, and two retail shops, we are dedicated to providing the best beauty products to our customers.

Our work process is currently offline, but we have a project in progress to establish a showroom where we will showcase and sell cosmetics, garments, and footwear. Once completed, we plan to expand our business online.

We offer a wide range of products for beautifying, preserving, or altering appearance, including skincare, makeup, and perfumes.

Perfumes: Giorgio Armani Aqua, Chanel Bleu de Chanel, Yves Saint Laurent, Versace Eros, Maison Louis Marie No.09, Calvin Klein CK, Notice Voyage, and local brands like Fogg, Set Wet, Denver, Marco, etc.
